# Sb(111) Growth Strategy

This public note summarizes the mechanism-guided Sb(111)-on-GaSb(111)A campaign design.

## Strategy

Develop matched growth comparisons for continuous, low-roughness Sb(111)-based films on III-V templates by varying:

- Sb source species: `Sb1`, `Sb2`, and `Sb4`
- template pretreatment and starting surface state
- substrate-temperature window
- nominal Sb thickness
- RHEED exposure state during early nucleation

## Scientific Purpose

The campaign is designed to separate coupled mechanisms that can otherwise be conflated across different growth days:

- group-V surface chemistry and incorporated Sb activity
- template-state effects on nucleation density and coalescence
- temperature-window effects on diffusion, desorption, and continuity
- possible RHEED perturbation of the earliest Sb nucleation stage

## Public Boundary

This note is a public-facing strategy summary. Raw growth-day notes, exact run histories, internal facility details, and unpublished characterization datasets remain private.
